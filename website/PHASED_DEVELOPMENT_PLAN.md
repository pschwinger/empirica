## Necessity Vector Tracking

Throughout development, track:
- **Completeness:** What pages/features are still missing?
- **Coherence:** Do all pages feel like one cohesive site?
- **Parsimony:** Are we over-engineering or duplicating?
- **Explicit Uncertainty:** What design decisions need human input?

Each phase ends with epistemic assessment before proceeding.
