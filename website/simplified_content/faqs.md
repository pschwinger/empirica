## Detailed Answers

### What are the 13 Vectors?
A comprehensive system to measure AI knowledge state, including **KNOW** (domain knowledge), **UNCERTAINTY** (explicit gaps), and **ENGAGEMENT** (task clarity). [Learn more](epistemics.md).

### How does CASCADE work?
A 7-phase workflow: **PREFLIGHT** -> **THINK** -> **PLAN** -> **INVESTIGATE** -> **CHECK** -> **ACT** -> **POSTFLIGHT**. It ensures thinking before acting.

### Can I use it offline?
**Yes.** Empirica works completely offline with local LLMs (Ollama) or cached sessions.

### Is it free?
**Yes.** Open source under MIT License.
