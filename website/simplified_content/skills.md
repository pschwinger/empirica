## How It Works

1. **Bootstrap:** Load skills based on profile.
2. **Ingestion:** AI reads semantic definitions.
3. **Application:** AI applies patterns to reasoning.
4. **Refinement:** POSTFLIGHT reflection.

### Example: Investigation Skill
> *Uncertainty > 0.4?* -> **Initiate Investigation Loop.**

## Developing Custom Skills

Create `.md` files with semantic structure:

```markdown
# Skill: Domain Analysis [ID: SKILL_001]
## WHAT
Decompose complex domains.
## HOW
1. Identify entities.
2. Map relationships.
## WHY
Prevents hallucination.
```
