üéØ Safety-First Philosophy

The Semantic Self-Aware Kit is designed with safety, reliability, and human oversight as core principles. We believe that advanced AI capabilities must be coupled with robust safety mechanisms, clear limitations, and transparent operation to build trustworthy systems.

Core Safety Principle

Human approval is required for all significant actions. The system is designed to be a powerful assistant that enhances human capability, not an autonomous actor that operates without oversight.
