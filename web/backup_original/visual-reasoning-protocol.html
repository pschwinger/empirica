Introduction

The Visual Reasoning Protocol (VRP) is a standard for human-interpretable AI state visualization, providing a direct window into the system's collective reasoning. VRP ensures that AI decision-making processes are transparent, auditable, and comprehensible to human operators in real-time.

Transparency Mandate

VRP is a cornerstone of the Sovereign AI Transparency Pillar, ensuring that AI reasoning processes are not black boxes. Every decision, state change, and uncertainty assessment must be visible and interpretable by human operators.
