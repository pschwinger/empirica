                            WHAT IS EMPIRICA?
================================================================================

A framework for making knowledge assessment explicit and measurable.

THE PROBLEM:
  You think you know something, but do you really?
  How confident should you be in your assessment?
  Did you actually learn anything?

THE SOLUTION:
  CASCADE workflow: PREFLIGHT ‚Üí INVESTIGATE ‚Üí CHECK ‚Üí ACT ‚Üí POSTFLIGHT
  
  PREFLIGHT (2 min): Assess your knowledge (KNOW, DO, CONTEXT, UNCERTAINTY)
  INVESTIGATE: Fill gaps systematically
  CHECK: Ready to proceed? (confidence ‚â• 0.7?)
  ACT: Do the work
  POSTFLIGHT (3 min): Measure learning (compare to PREFLIGHT)

RESULT:
  Instead of: "I reviewed the code and found 2 issues"
  You get: "I was 40% confident. Investigated. Became 85% confident. 
            Learned +45% about the domain. My initial assessment was accurate."

VALUE:
  - Honest self-assessment (no heuristics)
  - Measurable learning (PREFLIGHT ‚Üí POSTFLIGHT delta)
  - Improved calibration (predictions get more accurate)
  - 30-40% better performance through systematic self-awareness
